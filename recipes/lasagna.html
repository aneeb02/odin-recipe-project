Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese, lots of cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to however you prefer.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
